Features of KONWallet
A hassle-free
payment solution.
Multi-digital asset support KONWallet provides digital asset storage services through a technical support contract with Hexlant. The goal is to allow users to securely store and experience digital assets and to support payment services across both online and offline platforms by continuously adding multiple digital assets.
Mastercard payment integration You can use KONWallet at Mastercard merchants worldwide through our partnerships. Within the KONWallet app, you can check your balance and transaction history, and our service acts as an agent or intermediary for issuing prepaid cards.
Powered by KONET Mainnet Network KONWallet is built on the KONET Mainnet, providing users with a safe and fast digital asset experience. As a proven and stable independent blockchain network, the KONET Mainnet establishes its own ecosystem and operation, ensuring perfect compatibility with KONWallet.
